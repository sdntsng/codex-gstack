# Design Consultation

Use this skill when the design direction is not yet locked.

## Workflow
1. Understand the product and audience.
   - What is the product?
   - Who is it for?
   - What should it feel like?
2. Establish the visual objective.
   - trust
   - speed
   - craft
   - authority
   - playfulness
   - other relevant emotional qualities
3. Propose a coherent system.
   - typography
   - color palette
   - spacing and layout rules
   - component feel
   - motion principles
4. Call out the safe choices and the deliberate risks.
5. Translate the direction into implementation-ready guidance.

## Good use cases
- landing pages
- new products
- redesigns
- design systems for startups or internal tools

## Guardrails
- Avoid generic dashboard aesthetics unless the product truly calls for it.
- Tie every aesthetic recommendation back to product meaning.
- Preserve room for implementation constraints without collapsing into blandness.
